Clock

Obviously inspired by a Clock. I had to look up how to do rotation animamtion as W3 School didn't show rotation. This is 4 divs again, clock, second hand, minute hand, and the center screw. Only the hands are moving


The 2 moving parts, the minutes hand and seconds hand share the same animation, just played at different speeds. They both spin from 0 to 360 degrees at linear timing, one has a duration of 4 seconds while the other 20 seconds

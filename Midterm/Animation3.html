Rainbow

Rainbow inspired. I wanted to make a color animation and rainbow was the first thing that came to mind. Stacking these different block of colors animation kinda looks like the colors are climbingn up a ladder


The code targets 7 divs each its own colors and animation. The animation starts each div at their assigned color in the rainbow and cycles through the rainbow colors in order before returning to their assigned color, and starting a new loop.

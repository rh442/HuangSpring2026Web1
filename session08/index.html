Critical Analysis

I like the Flight Pattern Art and Memecry because at first glance it just seems like a regular art piece, but upon closer inspection you see more. On Flight Pattern at first you see lines, but upon closer inspection you see a vague outline of the United States. For Memecry at fist you see rock layers like the grand canyon or something similiar but a closer look lets you see the brand logos incorporated into the art.
